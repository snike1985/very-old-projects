Leading Strategic Growth

Issuse Forums are optional professor-moderated or coach-moderated group discussion sessions, where you can examine specific themes and topics relevant to the program and targeted to your personal case, professional function, or other areas of interest. You can ask questions to faculty and connect with other program participants with similar professional challenges and aspirations.

Below is the schedule of Issue Forums for your entire six-week program. Please save the dates on your calendar for the events which you would like to attend.
